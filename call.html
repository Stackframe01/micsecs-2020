Call for Papers

The Majorov International Conference on Software Engineering and Computer Systems (MICSECS 2020) is an international event dedicated to discussing research results and directions related to Computer Systems and Networks, Cyber-Physical Systems, Computer Security, Multimedia technologies, and AR/VR/MR technologies.

Conference languages: English and Russian.

Proceedings language: English.

The conference gives under- and post-graduate students a possibility to exchange their experience and results in perspective research and development of computer systems.

All authors will be required to pre-submit abstracts of their papers. All submissions will be carefully reviewed by the program committee. The acceptance rate in the 2019 year was 0.61.

Please refer to Registration to learn more about the registration procedure and the requirements for participation.

Important dates:

All deadlines are for the Moscow time zone, 23:59.

  • The begin of the participant registration: 5 October 2020
  • Paper submission: 5 November 2020
  • Conference program publication: 3 December 2020
  • Conference: 17-18 December 2020
  • Author notification: 30 November 2020
  • Camera-ready paper submission: 30 January 2021

Proceedings

The best articles will be selected for publication in Proceedings. The Proceedings will be available in printed and online versions.

We are going to publish the proceedings in CEUR with selectable DBLP, Scopus indexing.

Research Tracks

  • Computer systems design & development methodology
  • High-Performance Computing
  • Parallel & Distributed programming
  • Computer Networks and Telecommunication
  • Cloud Computing & Virtualization
  • Domain-specific modeling and metamodelling
  • Information Security and Privacy
  • Cryptography and Cryptanalysis
  • Computer Network Security
  • Digital Forensics and Cybercrime Investigation

Cyber-Physical Systems

  • Embedded Systems
  • Robotics
  • Internet of Things
  • Ubiquitous computing
  • Computer-Aided Design
  • Modeling and Co-simulation
  • Modern development tools (HLS, ESL, ASIP, etc.)

Software Engineering

  • Programming languages
  • Compilers and Toolchains
  • Artificial Intelligent & machine learning

Computer graphics and multimedia

  • Computer vision

AR/VR/MR technologies

  • Augmented, virtual, and mixed reality systems
  • Human-Computer Interaction & Interface

Student colloquium

In addition to oral paper presentations, MICSECS 2020 encourages Student colloquium.

The Student colloquium is dedicated to demonstrate work in progress projects of master's, bachelor's, and Ph.D. students focused more on practical and applied tasks, including their "pet" projects. It allows our participants to start networking in the early stages of their projects and career.

The best student presentation will get an award!
